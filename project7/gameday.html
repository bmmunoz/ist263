Getting to the Stadium

Yankee Stadium is located in the Bronx, New York City. The best way to get there is by subway. Take the 4, B, or D train to 161st Street - Yankee Stadium station. It’s fast, affordable, and avoids traffic.

Food and Drinks

Enjoy iconic stadium food like hot dogs, garlic fries, and specialty sandwiches. There are also options for vegetarians and gluten-free fans. Drinks include soft drinks, water, and a wide selection of local craft beers.

The Gameday Atmosphere

The crowd brings unbeatable energy to every game. Don’t forget your Yankees gear, camera for pictures, and a loud cheer for Judge or Cole. Be sure to visit Monument Park before first pitch!
