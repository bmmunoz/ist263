About the Yankees

The New York Yankees are a professional baseball team based in the Bronx, New York. Founded in 1903, they are one of the oldest and most iconic franchises in Major League Baseball. The Yankees are members of the American League East division. Known for their iconic pinstripes and legendary players, the Yankees symbolize tradition, success, and the heart of New York beyond sports. Yankee Stadium is where the Yankees currently call home.

Championship Glory

With 27 World Series titles, the Yankees are the most successful team in MLB history. Their championship runs in multiple eras, with legendary players leading the way in each generation. From the roaring 1920s to the dynasty of the late 1990s, Yankees fans have witnessed greatness time and time again. Rivalries

Few rivalries in sports are as intense as the one between the Yankees and the Boston Red Sox. Dating back more than a century, this rivalry has featured historic comebacks, heated debates, and unforgettable clashes. The inner-city rivalry with the New York Mets adds even more energy, during Subway Series games. And in recent years, matchups with the Tampa Bay Rays have also grown more competitive and emotional. Yankees rivalries bring out the passion of the team and its loyal fans.
