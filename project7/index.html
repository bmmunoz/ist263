About the Yankees

The New York Yankees are a professional baseball team based in New York City. They are a member of the American League (AL) East division in Major League Baseball (MLB).

Championship Glory

With 27 World Series titles, the Yankees are the most succesful team in MLB history.

  • Babe Ruth: The Sultan of Swat.
  • Lou Gehrig: The Iron Horse.
  • Joe DiMaggio: 56-game hitting streak legend.
